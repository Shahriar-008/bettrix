# Bettrix Website Design Spec

## Overview
Bettrix is a conversion-focused agency website for a web agency that sells affordable, fast website builds and ongoing maintenance. The primary goal is to drive discovery calls while building enough trust for visitors to move from the homepage to services, pricing, portfolio, and contact.

## Goals
- Present Bettrix as professional, fast, and affordable.
- Make it easy for visitors to understand services, pricing, and proof.
- Push a clear primary CTA: **Book a discovery call**.
- Support alternate contact via WhatsApp and form submission.

## Information Architecture
The site has 6 pages:
1. Home
2. Services
3. Portfolio
4. Pricing
5. About
6. Contact

Global elements:
- Sticky top navigation with CTA button.
- Sticky WhatsApp floating button on all pages.
- Consistent footer with quick links, contact details, and trust cues.
- Reusable CTA bands between major sections.

## Page Structure

### Home
1. **Hero** — Clear headline, supporting copy, primary CTA to book a discovery call, secondary CTA to view pricing.
2. **Trust strip** — Client logos, short stats, and trust badges.
3. **Services snapshot** — Cards for custom websites and maintenance.
4. **How it works** — 3-step process from discovery to launch.
5. **Portfolio highlights** — Featured case studies or project cards.
6. **Pricing preview** — Starter, Business, and Pro summary cards.
7. **Testimonials** — Short client quotes focused on speed, value, and communication.
8. **FAQ preview** — Common objections around cost, timeline, and maintenance.
9. **Final CTA** — Discovery call booking plus WhatsApp fallback.

### Services
1. **Hero** — What Bettrix builds and maintains.
2. **Custom website builds** — Scope, benefits, and typical outcomes.
3. **Website maintenance** — Updates, fixes, security, and support.
4. **Optional add-ons** — Extra support or growth services if needed.
5. **Process** — Discovery, design, build, launch.
6. **Why Bettrix** — Speed, affordability, clarity, and long-term support.
7. **CTA band** — Book a discovery call.

### Portfolio
1. **Hero** — Results-oriented intro to past work.
2. **Filter tabs** — Sort by business type or project type.
3. **Case studies grid** — Problem, solution, result.
4. **Outcome metrics** — Results such as speed, leads, or improved clarity where available.
5. **Testimonial block** — Proof tied to a project.
6. **CTA band** — Request a similar build.

### Pricing
1. **Hero** — Position the plans as affordable and easy to compare.
2. **Starter** — Best for simple launches.
3. **Business** — Best for growing companies needing more structure.
4. **Pro** — Best for teams needing the most complete solution.
5. **Comparison table** — Show included features across all plans.
6. **What’s included in every plan** — Baseline support and delivery expectations.
7. **Pricing FAQ** — Timeline, revisions, maintenance, and payment concerns.
8. **Final CTA** — Book a discovery call.

### About
1. **Hero** — Bettrix mission and positioning.
2. **Brand story** — Why the agency exists.
3. **Values** — Speed, clarity, affordability, reliability.
4. **Working process** — How clients move from kickoff to launch.
5. **Trust proof** — Stats, business types served, or client badges.
6. **CTA** — Contact or book a call.

### Contact
1. **Hero** — Simple reassurance and next step.
2. **Discovery call booking** — Main conversion path.
3. **Contact form** — Name, email, business type, message.
4. **WhatsApp + direct contact** — Alternate fast response path.
5. **Response promise** — Expected reply window.
6. **Final trust note** — Reduce hesitation and reinforce professionalism.

## Design System

### Visual style
- Clean, minimal, professional, card-based layout.
- White-first canvas with light gray surfaces.
- Minimal borders and subtle separators.
- Generous spacing and strong hierarchy.
- Subtle fade-in-on-scroll animation only.

### Color palette
- Background: White `#FFFFFF`
- Surface: Light Gray `#F9FAFB`
- Headings: Navy `#1E3A5F`
- Primary accent: Royal Blue `#1A56DB`
- Success / alternate CTA: Teal Green `#0E9F6E`
- Body text: Slate `#374151`

### Typography
- Font family: Inter
- Hero headings: 700
- Section headings: 600
- Body text: 400
- Labels / overlines: 600 uppercase

### Layout rules
- Mobile-first responsive layout.
- Cards stack vertically on mobile and expand into grids on desktop.
- Use strong whitespace to keep pages light and readable.
- CTA buttons should be visually distinct but not noisy.
- Trust elements should appear early and repeat near key decisions.

### Core components
- Hero section
- Trust strip
- Service cards
- Pricing cards
- Comparison table
- Case study cards
- Filter tabs
- Testimonials
- FAQ accordion
- CTA band
- Sticky header
- Sticky WhatsApp button

## Content and UX Rules
- Primary CTA across the site is **Book a discovery call**.
- Secondary CTA is **WhatsApp** or **View pricing** depending on context.
- The site should emphasize speed, affordability, and clarity over flashy visuals.
- Portfolio should focus on outcomes, not just visuals.
- Pricing should be transparent and easy to compare.
- FAQ should address cost, timing, revisions, maintenance, and fit.

## Animation and Motion
- Only use subtle fade-in on scroll.
- No parallax, autoplay, or distracting transitions.
- Motion should support clarity, not compete with content.

## Acceptance Criteria
- All 6 pages are defined with ordered sections.
- The design system matches the stated brand palette and typography.
- The site supports a clear discovery-call conversion path.
- Trust signals, pricing, FAQ, and WhatsApp access are included.
- The spec contains no placeholder sections or unresolved decisions.
